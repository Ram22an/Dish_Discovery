gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (Milk Solid)
  • All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Baking powder
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Milk or Water
  • Sugar Syrup

Recipe

  1. Prepare a soft dough using khoya, all-purpose flour, and a pinch of baking powder.
  2. Shape the dough into smooth balls.
  3. Heat ghee and fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with water, sugar, and flavoring.
  5. Soak the fried Gulab Jamun in the warm sugar syrup for 1-2 hours.

Milk Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Saffron

Recipe

  1. In a large pan, mix milk powder, sugar, and ghee. Cook on low heat, stirring continuously,
  2. Once the mixture thickens, add cardamom powder and mix well to infuse the flavor.
  3. Mix in the chopped nuts of your choice, reserving some for garnishing.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased tray or pan. Press it down evenly using a spatula.
  5. Allow the mixture to cool for a while. Once it's slightly firm.
