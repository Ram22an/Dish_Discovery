gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (Milk Solid)
  • All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Baking powder
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Milk or Water
  • Sugar Syrup

Recipe

  1. Prepare a soft dough using khoya, all-purpose flour, and a pinch of baking powder.
  2. Shape the dough into smooth balls.
  3. Heat ghee and fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with water, sugar, and flavoring.
  5. Soak the fried Gulab Jamun in the warm sugar syrup for 1-2 hours.
